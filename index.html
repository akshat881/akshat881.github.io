OUR VISION

our vision is to make people fall in love with coding, help them learn and practice to unlock new opportunities and create more employable talent.

OUR PLAN

We want to integrate learning with our organization as both a participant and a member. Along with technical skills, the organization will assist students in developing soft skills that will be essential to them throughout their career.

OUR MISSION

The organization was established to bridge the gap between learning and implementation by hosting a variety of programming, technology, and skill development-focused events. We strive to provide students with beneficial outcomes.

OUR MOTTO

We create opportunities for people who genuinely want to learn how they can make a career in coding. Skill and innovative thinking are the two most important things for a programmer and the confidence to transform their ideas into real-life products is what will help them succeed in life. We, at Coding Blocks LPU, aim to help our fellow coders develop their skills and be a part of something exceptional.

A little progress each day adds up to big results. 

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the various departments in this organization?


We have Graphic Designing, Video editing, Public Speaking, Technical Team, Event Management, Marketing, Social Media & Content writing.


Will members be promoted to higher positions?


Yes, members who stay active in the organization and work towards being a part of the organization's success will definitely be promoted.

Can I work in more than one domain?  


Yes, if the individual has the skillset or wants to work in more than one domain, then they are free to do so

How to join CodingBlocks LPU?


A recruitment drive will be conducted each semester to give a chance to the new & existing talent to be a part of our team

Does this organization work independently?


This student organization works under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University.

What is the eligibility criteria?


Any student belonging to Lovely Professional University can join. Students from all streams are free to join.
